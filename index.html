Our Story

Started all from a simple stall in Portobello market,
Pietro and Giovanni wanted to deliver fresh italian products for their contemporary pizza.
After two years of sacrifice, finally they realised that their dream to open a restaurant
were they give not only the good neapolitan pizza but also a great
customer service, could be really possible.
December 2019, finally TIPO was open to the public delivering
a biga dough with 16 hours of fermentaion, the best fiordilatte directly from Naples and the best italian products and wines.
